# `/kd-retrospective`

## Purpose
Post-mortem ringan untuk apa yang berjalan baik, apa yang perlu baiki, dan action items.

## Required Agents
- main-agent
- release-manager
- pm
- tech-lead

## Required Actions
1. Bezakan:
   - what went well
   - what went wrong
   - what to change next cycle
2. Hasilkan action items yang jelas.
3. Tulis output ke `KD_output/retrospective/retrospective.md`.
